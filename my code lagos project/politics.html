I don't believe Tinubu will stop me --Sanwo Olu

Sanwo Olu made the statement during an interactive session with newsmen in lagos

An All Progressive Congress governorship aspirant in lagos state, Mr Jide Sanwo-olu,on friday said he did not believe national leader of the party, Asiwaju Bola Tinubu, would ask hin to back down on his amibition

SANWO-OLU made the statement during an interactive session with newsmen in lagos

A journalist had asked him wheather he would step down his ambition or not if Tinubu asked him do so.

NAN reports that Sanwo-Olu, a former Commissioner for Training and Establishment, was reported to have been adopted by party chieftains loyal to Tinubu and the 57 council chairman to puncture the second term bid of Gov. AKINWUMMI AMBODE.

Ambode had declared his second-term bid on Monday, while Dr Obafemi Hamzat a former Commissioner for Works and Infrastructure had also obtained and submitted his nomination form for the race.

The state party chairman Alhaji Tunde Balogun had told the governorship ticker was open to all aspirants.

Sanwo-Olu told newsmen that he did not think Tinubu would ask him to step down for Ambode or any other aspirant as the party leader is a firm believer in internal democracy.
