We expect based on figures coming out of the registered voters to give him nothing less tham 70-80 percent. We don't have the figures now because they have completed additional registration, we are not talking about the last excercise that led to the election of 2015, and in 2015 he got almost 1.5millio votes from Katsina state.

So we expect by 2019 we will be able to give him additional one million making it not less than 2.5million votes.
