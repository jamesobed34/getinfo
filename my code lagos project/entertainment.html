Davido confirms cancellation of US tour due to NYSC commitment

Nigeria pop singer Davido on friday comfirmed the cancellation of his United States musical tour. The superstar disclosed that he cancelled his US tour to enable him conclude his National Youth Service Corps [NYSC] orientation programme. Davido is one of those mobilized for batch B of the service which is scheduled to end is three week orientation exercise on Monday, September 17.
